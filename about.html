About Lava Rock

15 years building Hawaii Island's strongest structures.

Est. 2009

Born from the Big Island

Derek and Kalani Makua founded Lava Rock Concrete in 2009 after years working for contractors who didn't understand Hawaii Island's unique challenges. Volcanic soil. Lava tube networks. Extreme UV. Seismic zones. These aren't things you learn on the mainland — they're learned by living here.

Over 500 projects later, we're the Big Island's most trusted concrete and masonry contractor. We work with lava rock the way our ancestors did — with respect for the land, genuine understanding of this geological environment, and pride in work built to last.

Every project gets a licensed engineer's review, proper drainage design, and materials selected for Hawaii Island conditions. We don't cut corners. We build for life.
