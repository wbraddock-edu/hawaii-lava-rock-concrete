Why Lava Rock

Big Island Specialists

Working with concrete on Hawaii Island requires expertise that mainland contractors simply don't have. Volcanic soil, seismic considerations, extreme UV, heavy tropical rain, and salt air all affect concrete work in ways that require island-specific knowledge.

  • Licensed Hawaii contractor (C-5 Concrete)
  • 15 years of Big Island project experience
  • Volcanic soil foundation specialists
  • UV-rated materials for Kona's intense sun
  • Engineered designs for seismic zones
